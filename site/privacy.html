Privacy Policy

For the Telegram bot @nabokov_editor_bot ("the bot"). Last updated: 24 July 2026.

The bot is operated by Viewflow (ТОО «Viewflow (Вьюфлоу)», BIN 220440008609) ("we", "us"). This page explains what data the bot handles and why. It is written to be read, not to hide anything.

What the bot does

You send the bot a piece of text. The bot edits it — it removes filler and AI-writing tells and restores rhythm — and sends the result back, usually with a before/after readability or AI-likeness score. That is the whole service.

What we process

  • The text you submit. We need it to produce the edit. See "Where your text goes" below.
  • Your Telegram user ID and, if set, your username. We use it to count your usage and apply your quota.
  • A usage counter — how many texts you have sent — so we can offer the free tier and paid quota correctly.

We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or location, and the bot does not collect them.

Where your text goes

To edit your text, the bot sends it to a third-party AI provider, DeepSeek (deepseek.com), whose model generates the rewrite. Your text leaves our systems for this processing and is subject to that provider's own terms and privacy handling. We send only the text you submit and no account data with it. Do not send secrets, credentials, or personal data you would not want processed this way.

We keep your submitted text only as long as needed to generate and return the reply. We do not sell it, and we do not use it to train models.

Payments

The first texts are free. After that the bot charges Telegram Stars. Payments are handled entirely by Telegram; we never see or store your card or payment details. We only learn that a Stars payment succeeded.

Who else sees data

  • Telegram — the messaging platform the bot runs on, under Telegram's own Privacy Policy.
  • DeepSeek — for the text-editing step, as described above.

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Retention

We store your Telegram user ID and usage counter for as long as you use the bot, so your quota stays consistent. You can ask us to delete this record (see Contact); after deletion your free tier simply resets.

Your choices

  • Stop using the bot at any time — no data is collected once you stop messaging it.
  • Ask us to delete your usage record, or ask what we hold about you, at the contact below.

Children

The bot is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly process their data.

Changes

If we change this policy we will update the date above. Continued use after a change means you accept it.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: mikhail@viewflow.io.
